About Us

At Sharda Overseas we are a trusted name in the import-export industry, based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. With a commitment to excellence and quality, we specialize in exporting a diverse range of high-quality industrial products and eco-friendly solutions.

Our product range includes customized gears, gearboxes, shafts, industrial machine rolls, timers, pulleys, and machinery parts, designed to meet the unique needs of various industries. We pride ourselves on delivering durable, precision-engineered products that ensure optimal performance.
